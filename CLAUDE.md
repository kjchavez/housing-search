## Accessing listing data

Listing portals (Zillow/Redfin/Apartments/Homes/Zumper) block automated fetching, and web-search
snippets are frequently wrong — **always read the live listing before trusting beds/rent/availability**
(e.g. 106 Oak was reported as a $1,050 room when it's actually a $5,500 house). Read pages with a real
browser and capture the facts + photos into each candidate's record. _(Detailed local tooling notes are
kept out of this repo.)_
